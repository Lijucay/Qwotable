<i>Qwotable</i> is an application, that has a ever growing collection of quotes.

Qwotable is using Google's latest Material 3 design language and is written in Kotlin - Jetpack
Compose.

Features:
<ul>
<li>Copy and Share quotes
<li>Mark quotes as favorite
<li>Random quotes (for certain APIs inside of the app and only on English and for a local database
on an app widget)
<li>Add your own quotes (all previously named features available + they are also shown on the app
widget)
